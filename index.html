About Me

My hometown is Cangzhou, Hebei, China.

When I was young my parents were teachers. I envy this profession, so I became a Chemistry teacher after graduating from university. I have been a teacher for 20 years in China, but I don’t like the Chinese Education system. There are so many students in a school, so it is hard for a teacher to consider every student’s needs and thoughts. I was tired as a teacher, and my son and daughter didn’t like to go to school as students. The links will help you learn more about the pressures we faced at that time.

I quit in 2022 and moved to Canada with my two children. It is a big challenge for me to restart in a new country at this age. I have to learn everything from the beginning. However, my children feel relaxed and happy in their school. They never refuse to go to school, and when I see their happy face every day I know moving to Canada is a wise decision.
